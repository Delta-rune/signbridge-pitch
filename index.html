Prototype Concept

SignVoice

Removing communication barriers with Real-Time AI for the Deaf & Mute community.

The Problem

Public interaction in banks, shops, and hospitals is often a struggle for deaf individuals. SignVoice uses AI to bridge this gap instantly.

How It Works

Sign Detection

Using MediaPipe & OpenCV to track hand landmarks and translate gestures to spoken audio.

Speech-to-Sign

Capturing shopkeeper speech and converting it into visual sign language animations.

Advantages

  • Independence in public hubs
  • Inclusive learning for hearing people
  • Low-cost mobile deployment
