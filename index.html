SignVoice

Removing communication barriers with Real-Time AI

Project Vision

SignVoice acts as a digital bridge between the deaf community and hearing individuals. By using computer vision and edge-AI, we enable natural conversation without needing a third-party translator.

The Strategy

Phase 1: Concept

Development of UI/UX and gesture tracking using MediaPipe and OpenCV.

Phase 2: Product

Deployment as a cross-platform Flutter app with offline voice recognition.

Key Advantages

  • Instant independence in shops and hospitals.
  • Reduced social isolation for mute individuals.
  • Zero-cost alternative to physical translators.
